Meet the Owners

Precious Sight is family owned and operated. Brian and Peggy have been married 31 years and counting. Brian is the administrator and spends most of his time managing accounts, crunching numbers and payroll. He also owns the catering business, Pork and Beans, that serves lunch to all locations. Peggy has over 20 years experience in child care beginning her career at Northern Kentucky Head Start. We have big hearts for children, including our own. We have five children and two grand children. Our oldest, Churstin, 30, is our Assistant Director at the Cold Springs location. Brian II "Andy", 27, cooks for Pork and Beans Catering and delivers lunch to all locations. Bryson is 10 and attends the school age program. Chance is 9 and also attends our school age program in Florence. Maci-Grace, 4, was adopted November 2020 and is a student in our Pre-K program in Florence. Our granddaughter, Nichcole is 11 (daughter of Churstin) and is active in cheerleading and gymnastics! Carter, 4 (son of Andy & Emily), attends our Pre-K program in Florence. Emily Spradlin (Andy's wife) is our Pre-K Director in the Florence location.
